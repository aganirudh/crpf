SECTION 4 — BID ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA (BEC)
============================================================================

The bidder shall fulfil ALL the following minimum eligibility criteria.
Failure to meet any single mandatory criterion will result in technical
disqualification.

4.1  FINANCIAL CAPACITY (Mandatory)
     The bidder shall have a minimum annual turnover of Rs. 5 (five) crore
     in any one of the last three (3) financial years (FY 2022-23, 2023-24,
     or 2024-25). Turnover shall be supported by:
       (a) audited financial statements signed by a practicing chartered
           accountant; AND
       (b) a CA certificate explicitly certifying turnover, bearing a valid
           UDIN.

4.2  TECHNICAL EXPERIENCE (Mandatory)
     The bidder shall have successfully completed at least three (3) similar
     construction projects of value not less than Rs. 2 (two) crore each in
     the last five (5) years as on the bid submission date. "Similar work"
     for the purpose of this tender shall mean construction of administrative,
     residential or institutional buildings for a Government department, PSU,
     or autonomous body.
     Evidence:
       (a) completion certificate issued by the project owner; AND
       (b) corresponding work order / agreement.

4.3  GOODS AND SERVICES TAX REGISTRATION (Mandatory)
     The bidder shall hold a valid GST registration. A copy of the GST
     Registration Certificate (Form GST REG-06) shall be submitted. The GSTIN
     must be active on the date of bid submission.

4.4  QUALITY MANAGEMENT SYSTEM CERTIFICATION (Mandatory)
     The bidder shall hold a valid ISO 9001 (Quality Management System)
     certification at the date of bid submission, issued by a certifying body
     accredited under the IAF Multilateral Recognition Arrangement.

4.5  PROVIDENT FUND / EMPLOYEES' STATE INSURANCE (Mandatory)
     The bidder shall be registered under the EPF & MP Act, 1952 and the
     ESI Act, 1948. Copies of registration certificates shall be submitted.

4.6  SAFETY MANAGEMENT (Desirable)
     The bidder is preferably ISO 45001 (Occupational Health & Safety)
     certified. While not mandatory for technical qualification, this will
     be considered favourably during evaluation.

4.7  BLACKLISTING DECLARATION (Mandatory)
     A self-declaration on company letterhead stating the bidder has not
     been blacklisted by any Government department, PSU, or autonomous body
     in India in the preceding 5 (five) years.
